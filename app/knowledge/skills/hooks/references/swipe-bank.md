## Conditionals (8)
- If you've launched twice and it flopped both times, read this.
- If your monthly churn is over 8%, your offer is leaking.
- If you have an audience of 10k+, you should never run paid ads first.
- If your sales page has 5+ scrolls, you've already lost them.
- If your offer doesn't have a guarantee, you're undercharging.
- If a member doesn't post in week 1, they're 80% likely to churn.
- If you don't know your dream-outcome value in dollars, you can't price.
- If your DMs are getting < 20% replies, the hook is wrong — not the offer.
